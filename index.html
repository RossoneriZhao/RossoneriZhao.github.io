Self Introduction

I'm Juntu Zhao, graduated with a bachelor's degree from the Artificial Intelligence Excellent Talents Pilot Class at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I am an outstanding graduate of Shanghai Jiao Tong University and started pursuing Ph.D. at Qingyuan Artificial Intelligence Research Institute at Shanghai Jiao Tong University in September 2023. My research focuses on the life sciences empowered by artificial intelligence.

I am served as the captain, assistant coach, and sprint team leader of the Shanghai Jiao Tong University Track-and-Field Team, the record holder for the men's 4x100m race at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, with a 100m lead of 11.03s. I won the silver medal in the men's 4x100m relay at the 2019 Shanghai University Athletics Championships and finished fourth in the men's 100m race at the 2022 Shanghai University Games.
