About

I am Juntu Zhao (赵峻图), a Ph.D. student in Artificial Intelligence at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

My research focuses on Embodied Intelligence, (especially Vision-Language-Action Models), multimodal LLMs, and I'm also interested in AI for Science.

I was graduated with a bachelor's degree from the Artificial Intelligence Excellent Talents Pilot Class at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I was an outstanding graduate of Shanghai Jiao Tong University and started pursuing Ph.D. at Shanghai Jiao Tong University in September 2023.
